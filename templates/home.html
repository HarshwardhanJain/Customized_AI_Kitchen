Welcome to AI Kitchen

AI Kitchen is an innovative application that helps you identify ingredients from images and provides recipes based on selected dishes.

Contributors:

Overview:

AI Kitchen simplifies the cooking process by leveraging AI technology. Users can select a dish, upload images of ingredients, and our application will classify the ingredients using a trained model. Based on the identified ingredients, AI Kitchen provides the corresponding recipe, making cooking easier and more efficient.
